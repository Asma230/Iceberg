Grounding Instructions

Welcome to the Emotion card game! Here's how to play:
There are 88 cards in this game, each with a unique emotion or feeling.
You can choose one card to explore in more depth.
There will be 5 questions to help you explore the emotion or feeling on the card.
You can also choose 5 cards to create your own "iceberg" of emotions and feelings.
Have fun and enjoy exploring your emotions!
